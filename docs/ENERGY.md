# Dark Force (DF) Energy System

DF is Valence's end-game energy system, built on top of Forge Energy (FE).

## How It Works

DF energy is fully compatible with Forge Energy (IEnergyStorage). Machines
that generate, store, or consume DF work with any FE-compatible block from
other mods.

## Tiers

| Machine | Capacity | Transfer Rate |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| DF Cell | 1,000 DF | 20 DF/tick |
| Mega Cell | 100,000,000 DF | 20 DF/tick |

(Values can be tweaked in `valence-common.toml`)

## Generation

DF is generated by the **Steam Turbine**:
- Consumes steam (produced by Steam Dynamo from water)
- Outputs DF energy to adjacent machines/cables
- Requires a Steam Dynamo, boiler setup, and Water Collector

## Transmission

DF flows between adjacent blocks automatically:
- **Transfer Conduit** — basic DF wire, 2 per craft
- **Energy Cable** — upgraded DF wire, 4 per craft
- **Wireless Node** — transmits DF across any distance (needs Linking Tool to pair)

All DF-capable machines (DF Cell, Electric Furnace, Wireless Node, etc.)
push/pull energy to/from neighbors each tick. No manual configuration needed
for simple chains — just place machines next to each other.

## Storage

- **DF Cell:** 1,000 DF buffer — good for early electric setups
- **Mega Cell:** 100 million DF — end-game mass storage

## Consumption

| Machine | Consumption | Per |
|---------|------------|-----|
| Electric Furnace | 5 DF/tick | While smelting |
| Seed Duplicator | 50 DF/tick | Per operation |
| Tree Growth Chamber | Varies | Per growth tick |

## Config

All DF values are configurable in `valence-common.toml` under the `[energy]` section.
